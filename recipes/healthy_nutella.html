Healthy Nutella

Homemade sugar-free healthy nutella

This recipe tastes just as good as the original Nutella but it's completely sugar free. It's also much healthier than the traditional kind: no palm oil, no dairy and no artificial ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Roasted hazelnuts
  • Coconut sugar
  • Cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Toast your hazelnuts.
  2. Remove the loose skins.
  3. Blend the hazelnuts.
  4. Add the cocoa powder and sugar substitute.
  5. Blend.
  6. Transfer your homemade chocolate spread into a jar.
