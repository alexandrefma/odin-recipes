Healthy Nutella

Homemade sugar-free healthy nutella

This recipe tastes just as good as the original Nutella but it's completely sugar free. It's also much healthier than the traditional kind: no palm oil, no dairy and no artificial ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Roasted hazelnuts
  • Coconut sugar
  • Cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Toast your hazelnuts.
  2. Step 2: Remove the loose skins.
    3. Step 3: Blend the hazelnuts.
  3. Step 4: Add the cocoa powder and sugar substitute.
  4. Step 5: Blend.
  5. Step 6: Transfer your homemade chocolate spread into a jar.
